Why This Design Works

The "C" Shell — Chat-Window Shape

The open rectangular C doubles as a conversation window, signaling that CrafterIQ is a conversational AI platform. The open right side invites interaction — data flows in, intelligence flows out. Rounded corners and the chat tail ground it as approachable yet professional.

Three Ascending Bars — Analytics DNA

Short, medium, tall — the bars encode growth and data analysis at a glance. Placed inside the C, they show analytics is the core of the product. Progressive opacity (100% → 68%) adds dimensionality without clutter. This is the "Crafter" part: we craft insights from raw data.

Growth Trend Curve — Upward Trajectory

The sweeping curve from bottom-left to top-right is the universal symbol for growth and positive outcomes. It crosses all three bars, unifying them into a single narrative: your spend data trends upward toward savings and optimization. The gradient (light blue → accent blue) reinforces forward momentum.

AI Sparkle Star — Intelligence Signal

The 4-point star with glow center is the industry-recognized AI indicator. Positioned at the top-right of the C, it marks this as an AI-powered platform without being heavy-handed. The micro sparkle and dot accent add depth and polish, signaling precision and sophistication — the "IQ" in CrafterIQ.

Navy Primary (#1B2B65) — Trust & Authority

Deep navy conveys enterprise-grade trust, institutional authority, and financial seriousness. It says "we handle your money data responsibly." This is the shell, the tall bar, the foundation — used for primary UI elements, text, and navigation. It outperforms generic black by adding warmth and depth.

Electric Blue (#2D7FF9) — Action & Intelligence

The vibrant accent blue signals technology, AI, and actionable intelligence. It's used for the "IQ" in the wordmark, CTAs, active states, and interactive elements. It creates instant visual hierarchy against the navy and provides the energy the brand needs — smart, modern, and decisive. Avoids the overused purple-AI cliché.

Sky Blue (#5AB0FF) — Clarity & Optimism

The lighter blue serves as the gradient endpoint, providing visual breathing room and optimism. It's used in bar highlights, trend start points, and micro sparkles. Combined with the electric blue in gradients, it creates a sense of progression — from data (raw) to insight (refined). This gradient pair is the signature of the brand.

Slate (#94A3B8) — Supporting & Secondary

The neutral slate handles all supporting text, labels, timestamps, and subtle UI elements. It prevents visual competition with the primary navy and accent blue, keeping the hierarchy clean. Used for the tagline, descriptions, and metadata — present but never dominant.

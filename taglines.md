# CrafterIQ Tagline Options

## Primary (Recommended)

### "Ask. Understand. Decide."
The three-word cadence maps directly to the product journey:
natural language question -> AI-generated insight -> data-driven decision.
Clean, active, executive-level. Works in hero, email signatures, pitch decks.
